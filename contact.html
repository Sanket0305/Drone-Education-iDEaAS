Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a drone training program?

You can enroll by filling out our online application form, contacting our admissions office via phone or email, or visiting our campus in person. Our team will guide you through the enrollment process and help you choose the right program.

What are the prerequisites for drone pilot training?

Basic programs require no prior experience, though familiarity with technology is helpful. Advanced programs may require completion of our basic course or equivalent experience. Minimum age is 18 years for certification.

Do you provide placement assistance after training?

Yes, we have a dedicated placement cell that assists graduates with job opportunities in various industries. We also provide career counseling and help with resume building and interview preparation.

Can I visit the campus before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to visit our campus, meet our instructors, and see our facilities. Please contact us to schedule a visit during our office hours.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and bank transfers. We also offer installment plans for some programs.
