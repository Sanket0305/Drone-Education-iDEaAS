Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to enroll in drone courses?

Requirements vary by course level. For beginner courses, you typically need to be at least 18 years old with basic computer skills. Advanced courses may require prior drone experience or completion of prerequisite courses. Some specialized programs may have additional requirements which will be listed in the course details.

Do I need to bring my own drone?

No, all necessary equipment is provided for training. We have a fleet of training drones suitable for different course levels. However, if you have your own drone, you're welcome to bring it for certain training sessions after consulting with your instructor.

Are your courses certified by DGCA?

Yes, iDEaAS is an approved Remote Pilot Training Organisation (RPTO) by the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA). Our certification courses meet all regulatory requirements for commercial drone operations in India.

What career opportunities are available after completing these courses?

Graduates can pursue careers as commercial drone pilots, aerial photographers, industrial inspectors, surveyors, mapping specialists, or drone technicians. Opportunities exist in industries like agriculture, construction, media, mining, and public safety. We also provide placement assistance to our graduates.

Is financial aid or payment plans available?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including installment plans for most courses. Some programs may qualify for education loans through our banking partners. Contact our admissions team for details on available financial assistance options.
