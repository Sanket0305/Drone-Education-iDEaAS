About iDEaAS

Pioneering drone education and aerospace training for the future

Our Story

Shaping the Future of Drone Technology

Founded in 2018, the Institute of Drone Education and Aerospace (iDEaAS) was established with a vision to bridge the growing gap between drone technology advancements and skilled professionals in the field. What began as a small training center has now grown into a premier Remote Pilot Training Organisation (RPTO) recognized by aviation authorities.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, adapting our curriculum to meet the evolving needs of industries leveraging drone technology. From our first batch of 12 students to now training hundreds of certified drone pilots annually, we've remained committed to our core values of excellence, safety, and innovation.

Today, iDEaAS stands at the forefront of drone education, with state-of-the-art facilities, experienced instructors, and industry partnerships that ensure our graduates are ready to meet the challenges of tomorrow's aerospace landscape.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide world-class drone education and training that equips students with the technical skills, regulatory knowledge, and practical experience needed to excel in the rapidly evolving aerospace industry. We are committed to fostering innovation while maintaining the highest standards of safety and professionalism.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as the premier institute for drone education, shaping the future of aerospace technology by developing skilled professionals who drive innovation across industries. We envision a world where drone technology is safely and effectively integrated into everyday life, with iDEaAS graduates leading the way.

Our Values

Excellence: In training, technology, and outcomes
Innovation: Pushing boundaries in drone applications
Safety: Our top priority in all operations
Integrity: Ethical practices in all we do
Community: Building connections that drive progress
